EMPLOYMENT

Linville Ridge Country Club is an equal opportunity employer. We offer full and part time, year round and seasonal positions. Our club operation employs coworkers in numerous departments including:

  • Golf Course Maintenance
  • Golf Operations
  • Aquatics
  • Tennis
  • House Maintenance
  • Housekeeping
  • Numerous Member Service departments

Linville Ridge Country Club values its employees and considers them as one of the most important assets of our company. Everyone adds value to our team and shares in our success.If you are interested in more information, please fill out the Employment Application.
