MANAGEMENT & STAFF

Walther Vliegen
General Manager
Walther Vliegen is the General Manager at Linville Ridge and is originally from Maastricht, Netherlands. With 45 years of experience in the field, Walther has spent most of his career in the hospitality industry and has held upper-level management positions in renowned hotels, resorts and country clubs across North America and Western Europe. Fluent in French, Dutch and German, Walther has earned several hotel and general management designations. In his spare time, he enjoys mountain biking, sports car racing and travelling.

Adam Shirah
Food & Beverage Director

Adam Shirah comes to the mountains from Jacksonville, FL. Adam worked for ten years in casual dining prior to joining the team at TPC Sawgrass as the Assistant Food and Beverage Director. At TPC, Adam was part of the team that opened the re-built clubhouse in 2007. While at TPC his focus was overseeing the three restaurant venues, on course food and beverage, as well as private dinner functions. In November of 2010 Adam successfully completed coursework and exams to reach Level II Sommelier certification from the Court of Master Sommeliers. When he is not working, Adam can be found at home with his wife Kelly, fly fishing, playing music, rooting for his alma mater Florida Gators, or traveling to his next wine country destination.

Alisa Finney-Lucas
Property Services

Originally from Plantation, Florida, Alisa is a Florida State University graduate with a Bachelor of Science in interior design. Since graduating she has worked in the design field for 12 years and spent another 16 years in office management. Before moving to NC, Alisa worked for one of the top 25 Design Firms in South Florida – Pavlik Design Team.  She also worked for Adache Associates Architect, Design Associates of Boca Raton, Designer Ira Cohen, and Robert Allen at the Design Center of the Americas.   Alisa is a past president and current board member of the Avery County Humane Society. In her spare time, she enjoys antiques, camping, gardening and interior design.

Bernhard Smit
Executive Chef

Bernhard Smit was born in Mannheim, Germany into a family of chefs dating back several hundred years. Following his formal culinary education at Speiser’s Hotel & Culinary School, in Tegernsee, Germany, with internships in Munich, Germany and Kitzbuhel, Austria at the Sheraton, Hilton, and Bayerischer Hof Hotels, Smit began his career in the United States leading to earning the designation as a Certified Executive Chef by the American Culinary Federation in 2000.  

His career includes culinary management positions at the Grove Park Inn & Country Club in Asheville NC, Ocean Creek Resort in Myrtle Beach SC, and Epping Forest Yacht Club in Jacksonville In 1990, Smit opened and served as Executive Chef at The Plantation Country Club in Ponte Vedra Beach FL, where he worked for over seven years before opening, operating and after seven years selling his own restaurant, Uli’s European Restaurant, in Jacksonville Beach, FL. In 2005 he was appointed Executive Chef at Amelia Island Club, in Amelia Island FL where he delighted members with his finely honed skills.

David Maloof
Croquet Professional

David Maloof has competed nationally in croquet for the past decade and has placed as high as 3rd place in the National Croquet Championships in the American Six-Wicket game, as well as the International Rules Competition. During the winter, David resides in Florida where he is the director of tennis and croquet at the Mountain Lake Club in Lake Wales. David returns to Linville Ridge for his 10th season this summer.

Denise Brown
Spa Director/Massage Therapist

Denise Brown is a Detroit native who has lived in Boone for over 25 years. A licensed massage and bodywork therapist, a North Carolina Realtor and a licensed General Residential Builder, Denise has worked full time in the spa setting, while also subcontracting through Linville Ridge. She is an Appalachian State University alumna and is actively involved in the Boone Service League and the Boone Rotary Club. In May of 2013, the Boone Area Chamber of Commerce recognized Denise for her “Amazing Customer Service.” Denise has two daughters, Madison and Allison.

Heather Dockweiler 
Director of Marketing
Heather Dockweiler was born and raised in Memphis, Tennessee. Upon graduating from high school she attended the University of Arizona in Tucson, Arizona where she received a Bachelor of Science degree in Business and Public Administration and participated in Semester at Sea, a Travel-Study Program. She then went on to Oglethorpe University in Atlanta, Georgia and graduated magna cum laude with a Masters in Business Administration. 

In 2002, Heather moved to Naples, Florida to be closer to her family. She spends her time between Naples, Florida and Linville, North Carolina working as the Director of Marketing for Linville Ridge.
 
Heather volunteers for various charitable organizations.  She is a Founding Guild Member of the Golisano Children’s Museum of Naples served as the 2007 Chairman and a 2005, 2006 and 2008 committee member for the Hats in the Garden Luncheon which benefits the Naples Botanical Garden; an event coordinator for the 2005 to 2007 Naples Winter Wine Festivals which benefit the Naples Children & Education Foundation; and a member of the Collier County Heart Walk’s Logistic Committee from 2004 to 2007 which benefits the American Heart Association. Currently she is serving as a Board Member of the Naples International Film Festival.

Heather was recognized as one of Southwest Florida’s accomplished young leaders in 2005’s 40 Under Forty by Gulfshore Business magazine and was chosen as a member of the Leadership Collier Class of 2007 which is put on by The Greater Naples Chamber of Commerce.

Heather enjoys spending time with her family and friends, traveling, photography, writing and doing graphic design projects. She married James “Jamie” Dockweiler in May 2007 and together they have two children.

Jamie Dockweiler
VP of Membership

A graduate of Kennesaw State University with a degree in Sports Management, prior to moving to Naples, Jamie worked in marketing management for SFX Sports Group/Clear Channel Communications out of Atlanta, Georgia. In 2002, Jamie and his wife Heather moved to Naples, Florida to be closer to family and he began to teach at Royal Palm Academy and coach varsity basketball for Naples High School. 

Jamie is actively involved in the community supporting various charitable organizations including the Naples Winter Wine Festival benefitting the Naples Children and Education Foundation, the American Heart Association, the Naples Botanical Garden, the Golisano Children's Museum of Naples, the Naples International Film Festival and the Shelter for Abused Women and Children. Jamie was awarded recognition in Gulfshore Business's 40 Under 40 in 2009. 

Jamie and his family spend their summers in Linville Ridge. Because of his passion for the community, the golf course and his relationships with the club members the company hired him to serve as the Vice President of Membership. 

In his spare time, Jamie enjoys sports, playing golf and tennis, movies and film.

Karen Caroll
Assistant Manager

Karen, a Pittsburgh, PA native (and loyal Steelers fan), came to Linville Ridge from West Palm Beach, FL in 1990.
