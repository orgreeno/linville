NEIGHBORHOODS

Branch Water Run
Branch Water Run boasts single-family estate homes with long range mountain, vista and golf course views. The homes, which average 4,000 square feet on 3 acres, sit at 4,600 feet above sea level. All are golf cart accessible and close to dining, clubhouse, spa and fitness center. This area culminates in the exclusive enclave know as “West Ridge”, a bastion of privacy.   Branlaire
Located at the top of the mountain at an elevation of 4,900 square feet, and boasting long range views including Grandfather Mountain and the signature 16th hole of the golf course, Branlaire is comprised of a limited number of custom single-family homes and an eight-unit condominium area. Floor plans range from two to four bedrooms, and yield an average square footage of 2,000. Accessible by golf cart, this neighborhood is very convenient to the golf course, clubhouse, restaurants, spa and fitness center.Bridgeway/Vista Way
Accessed by its landmark stone bridge, Bridgeway and Vista Way offer quaint single-family mountain cottages with Grandfather Mountain views at a 4,100 feet elevation. Particularly suited to those who enjoy tennis, croquet, dining and the outdoor pool, residences in this area have a minimum of three bedrooms and average 3,000 square feet, with property that averages 3 acres.

BrookhillWithin walking distance to golf, dining, spa, clubhouse and fitness center, Brookhill is a condominium neighborhood at an elevation of 4,600 feet. Sixteen residences, offering two or three bedrooms and an average of 1,800 square feet of living space, are located within four buildings nestled between the 1st green and the 9th fairway. Brookhill residents enjoy golf course, Grandfather Mountain and vista views.

Chestnut Trail
Located off lower Ridge Drive, single-family estate homes at Chestnut Trail offer incredible vista and mountain views. Situated on a minimum of 2 acres at an elevation of 4,100 feet, these homes have three to four bedrooms and an average of 3,500 square feet of living space. Tennis, croquet, pool and Belvedere dining are readily accessible from this neighborhood.

Club Cliffs
This area enjoys long range mountain views, as well as, vista and Grandfather Mountain views. All Club Cliff homes are grand estates with an average of 4200 square feet on an average of 3 acres. There are a total of five sites; this area is private – yet very convenient to dining, tennis, croquet and pool.

Cranberry Cove
Cranberry Cove is Linville Ridge’s newest neighborhood of luxury town homes. Situated at an average elevation of 4,000, this area offers Grandfather and other mountain views. With an average square footage of 2,600, both three and four bedroom plans – some with guesthouse – are available. Each townhome is situated on a minimum of 1 acre, and is convenient to tennis, croquet, pool and dining.

Crest TrailCombining single-family estate homes, town homes and luxury condominiums averaging 3,500 square feet, Crest Trail is situated between holes 11 and 12, offering compelling views from its 4,800 elevation. All single family homes are placed on an average of 1 acre, and overlook the golf course and Grandfather Mountain. In addition, this neighborhood offers an enjoyable walking trail that extends into the adjacent Moon Run. This area is golf cart accessible and within walking distance to golf, spa, fitness center, clubhouse and dining.

Crestview
Top of the mountain living at an elevation of 4,800 feet is available in this neighborhood of town homes and condominiums. Golf cart accessible and within walking distance of the Clubhouse, golf course, spa and fitness center, Crestview offers three and four bedroom plans averaging 2,700 square feet.

Dam Trail
Dam Trail residents can enjoy golf course access as well as vista and Grandfather Mountain views. With an average lot size of 2 acres at an elevation of 4,700 feet, most estates average four bedrooms. This area is convenient to golf, fitness center, spa, clubhouse and dining.

Grouse Moor
Consisting of single-family homes as well as twelve condominiums in three buildings, this neighborhood boasts Grandfather and mountain views at an elevation of 4,200 square feet. All condominiums offer three bedrooms, while single-family homes contain a minimum of three bedrooms. All are within walking distance to tennis, croquet, pool and dining.

Moon Run
Known for its walkability, Moon Run is a private street of estate homes paralleling the golf course. These residences, which sit at an average elevation of 4,100 feet, average 4,800 square feet on 1 acre lots. The community clubhouse, dining, spa, fitness and golf amenities are all within walking distance of this neighborhood

Mountain View
This neighborhood consists of single-family cottages, each with three bedrooms and a minimum of 1,500 square feet, at an average elevation of 4000 feet. Featuring Grandfather, vista and mountain views, Mountain View residences are within walking distance to tennis, croquet, pool and dining.

Oak Ridge
At an elevation of 4,600 feet, this neighborhood claims top of the mountain location and long-range mountain and golf views. One single-family estate home and multiple luxury town homes include three bedroom and four bedroom floor plans averaging 3,800 square feet, on property averaging one acre. Oak Ridge residents are able to walk or ride golf carts to the golf, clubhouse, spa, fitness and dining facilities.

Ridge Drive
The most diverse street that Linville Ridge has to offer, Ridge Drive traverses the entire mountain, in essence completing a circle around the Ridge. All residences are single family homes with an average square footage of 3,800, but elevation varies 4,200 feet to 4,848 feet. With an average lot size of 5 acres, these homes offer a variety of views, including vista, golf course, long range mountain and Grandfather Mountain.

Rock Ledge Lane
Rock Ledge Lane offers flat, walkable streets shaded by tall native trees. All homes are single-family residences located on wooded lots that average one acre and offer three bedrooms and over 3,000 square feet of living space. Here, residents are close to tennis, croquet, dining, and the outdoor heated pool.

Split Rock Estates
An indigenous split rock marks the entry to this very unique area of Linville Ridge. Here, estate homes average 7,000 square feet on properties ranging from 7 to more than 30 acres at an average elevation of 4,500 feet. Split Rock views follow the mountains, past Grandfather and into Virginia and Tennessee.

Summit
As its name suggests, this neighborhood sits at the pinnacle of the ridge at an elevation above 5,000 feet. Just nine estate homes will grace the Summit, and each will benefit from expansive vistas in every direction, from long range mountain views to overlooks of the golf course and Grandfather Mountain. Convenient to golf, dining, clubhouse, spa and fitness center, these residences boast an average property size of 2 acres, and are golf cart accessible.

Water Court
With single-family cottages averaging three bedrooms and 1,800 square feet, Water Court homes are zero lot line to minimize owner maintenance and maximize natural areas. Located at an average 4,000 feet elevation, these residences are readily accessible to tennis, croquet, dining, and the pool area.

Woodlands
Featuring single-family cottages and town homes, Woodlands residences average three bedrooms and 2,000 square feet, all at an average elevation of 4,150. Tennis, croquet, dining and pool facilities are all easy to access from this neighborhood.
