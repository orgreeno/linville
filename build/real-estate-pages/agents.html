Natalie Watson 
Managing Broker – Real Estate Broker

Natalie, a true area native, began working at Linville Ridge upon graduation from high school in 1995. While at “The Ridge”, she continued her education and graduated from Appalachian State University in Boone, North Carolina in 1999. She holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration with a double major in Real Estate and Finance.  

She has assisted many of the current residents in selecting their mountain home. As an on-site sales specialist in Linville Ridge, her experience and knowledge of the High Country and Linville Ridge is of great value to clients in search of the perfect mountain retreat. She prides herself on asking the right questions, communicating the next steps and moving transactions forward. She has been an active member of the High Country Association of REALTORS® since 1999.  

During the winter months, Natalie spends a great deal of time in Naples, Florida working for the Naples Winter Wine Festival, which benefits the Naples Children & Education Foundation. She has been working for this charitable cause since 2006. In her spare time, she enjoys volunteering, playing golf and traveling. 

Jonathan Rivers 
Leasing Director – Broker Associate

Jonathan has been working at Linville Ridge since 2002. Before joining the real estate team, he worked in Linville’s Food and Beverage service for 10 years. Jonathan gained valuable customer service experience working with residents and members at Linville.   

As an Avery county native, he is familiar with the local market. He attended Cape Fear Community College in Wilmington, North Carolina and earned his Associate in Arts degree.   

In his spare time, he likes to snowboard and travel. He has been a volunteer at the Naples Winter Wine Festival for many years.   

Karen Barker 
Broker Associate – SRES

Karen Barker specializes in the residential real estate markets of Blowing Rock and Boone. Her success underlies an unflagging devotion in serving her client's interests. That commitment provides the highest level of professional service when achieving a dream home in the mountains. Karen educates her clients to the economy's real-time changes; real estate decisions become influenced by market information and professional insight. Her philosophy: it's not enough to drive people around and unlock doors; a real estate pro must be prepared with information, insight and skill. 

Karen has two children, Jackson and Suzanne, and three dogs. She participates in Blowing Rock Leadership Challenge and volunteers with Blowing Rock Wine Festival, the Symphony at Chetola, as well as in her children's schools and church youth groups.
