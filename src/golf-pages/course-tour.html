Hole 1

Par 5
Yards: 580

A drawn tee shot will follow the slight right to left fairway putting you in a good position on this opening par 5. Players will have the option to lay up for a full approach shot or take their chances in attacking this hole. Strategic bunkers short and left of the green complex will challenge the errant lay up shot. Be careful not to be long or left on your approach as these areas call for a demanding up and down.
