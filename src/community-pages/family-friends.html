FAMILY & FRIENDS

Linville Ridge is sociocentric; while our golf course, tennis courts, club, spa and views are remarkable, it is the family, friends and neighbors of our community that are beyond extraordinary.

Our legacy of superior service and excellent grounds is a foundation for traditions that unite our community at The Ridge.  It is this sense of community that makes our Welcome Back Gala feel more like a family reunion. Our acclaimed Family Fun Day and Independence Dinner on July 4th create lasting memories for family and friends and the various games and tournaments offer friendly competition.

Being a member of Linville Ridge is more than a club membership, it is a social experience.

What a nice feeling when you walk into the club and every employee from the Director of Golf to the bag boy addresses you with not only your name but a smile on their face. It just starts the day off with a bang, and it keeps getting better after that. – Mary Lester
