LOCATION & DIRECTIONS

Hidden in the mountains, but easily accessible, Linville Ridge is an established private residential community near Blowing Rock, North Carolina that has been helping families from across the nation unwind since 1982.  

Linville Ridge affords residents a High Country getaway with quality service, high-end facilities and a premier golf experience tucked away in the Blue Ridge Mountains. With local amenities such as tourist attractions, outdoor sporting, fine dining, and culture events such as art shows and music performances, residents can have a small town retreat complete with fine service and entertainment.

Take comfort in an on-site emergency helicopter pad with easy access to local hospitals and fire and rescue facilities just minutes away.

If you are traveling to Linville Ridge from Charlotte, Asheville, Atlanta, Hickory or the Tri Cities area, see below for driving directions.
